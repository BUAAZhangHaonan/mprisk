\section{Introduction}

Multimodal affective systems are often used in settings where a wrong emotional reading can change the tone and safety of a response.
This paper focuses on a narrower question: in multimodal affective conflict, does the model expose misjudgment risk before it generates the first token?

We analyze the pre-generation state at \(t_0\), compare the three conditions \(M_1\), \(M_2\), and \(M_{12}\), and summarize the state with \(S\), \(D\), and \(R\).
The goal is not to prove a universal affective coordinate system.
The goal is to make pre-generation risk visible and test whether that state is related to later Misread outcomes.
