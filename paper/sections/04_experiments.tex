\section{Experiments}

\subsection{Experimental Setup}

The registered backbones are Qwen3-VL-8B and InternVL3.5-8B under V--T, and
Qwen2.5-Omni-7B under V--A. All figures use held-out \texttt{official\_test} samples.
The thresholds are fitted only on the independent Aligned calibration split and are
bound to the model, protocol, prompt set, encoder checkpoint, split assignment, and
embedding manifest. Three prompt seeds are treated as repeated measurements rather than
as independent samples.
